Welcome to the help page of Todofic, here you can find everything you need to know about this app!

  1. Menu - In the menu, you can select settings, which will take you to a page with some color themes for you to choose from!

  2. More options - When you click on the more options button (three dots on top of each others) you will see 2 options : Help, About. You may see the "Select all" & the "Clear all" options, but these will only appear when you add a new task!

  3. Add task - To add a new task, just click on the plus button on the bottom right corner of the screen.

  4. Add task screen - On the add task screen, you wil find three input forms: description, category, due date and time. Fill these and click on the send button on the top right corner of the screen.

If you have any questions or if you want to report a bug, email me here!
