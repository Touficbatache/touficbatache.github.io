Welcome to the help page of Secondific, here you can find everything you need to know about this app!

  1. Menu - In the menu, you can select settings, which will take you to a page with some color themes for you to choose from!

  2. More options - When you click on the more options button (three dots on top of each others) you will see 2 options : Help, About.

  3. Calculator - To calculate the number of seconds you lived till now, just click on the "Calculate" button on the bottom of the date chooser.
